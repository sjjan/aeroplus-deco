GF99 — TISSUE SATURATION

Negative = on-gassing  ·  0% = equilibrium  ·  Positive = off-gassing  ·  Target GF99 = GF(hi)/2

About GF99: GF99 is the percentage of the M-value currently in use across all tissue compartments. A negative value means you are on-gassing. Zero is equilibrium. A positive value means you are off-gassing. The recommended target during deco stops is approximately 50% of your GF(hi) setting (shown as the blue dashed Target GF99 line).

Rock bottom & TTS

Rock bottom reserve: Minimum bottom gas for 2 divers at emergency SAC to ascend from max depth to the first deco gas switch depth, including the gas switch pause.

Ascent trigger

Start your ascent when either your dive computer shows TTS ≤ your target, or your gas reaches the minimum reserve — whichever comes first.

EAD — He inert, O₂ non-narcotic (most agencies). END — O₂ narcotic (GUE). EADD — density-equivalent depth.
CCR loop: the entered mix is your diluent. At the setpoint the unit adds O₂, so the loop O₂ fraction = setpoint ÷ absolute pressure, with the diluent’s He/N₂ scaled to fill the rest. Loop gas density is what you actually breathe — it can sit above or below the diluent’s, since O₂ is heavier than the He it displaces.

About
AeroPlus Deco
Technical dive decompression planning.
AeroPlus Deco is a technical dive decompression planner for open-circuit and closed-circuit rebreather diving, built on the Bühlmann ZHL-16C-GF algorithm. Developed by Magnolia Blossom BV, Doesburg, The Netherlands.
Core algorithm
✓  Bühlmann ZHL-16C with gradient factors (GF Lo / GF Hi)
✓  Optional Auto GF Low tied to deepest deco-gas switch
✓  Trimix (N₂ + He + O₂) with helium tracking
✓  Fresh / salt water and altitude compensation
✓  Configurable ascent and descent rates
Open circuit
✓  Multi-gas planning with automatic deco-gas switching
✓  Travel gas with calculated switch depth
✓  Hypoxic bottom-gas safety check
✓  Rock-bottom (minimum gas) reserves per cylinder
✓  Gas density warnings (5.2 / 6.2 g/ℓ thresholds)
Closed circuit (CCR)
✓  Loop setpoints: surface / switch-depth / bottom / deco
✓  Per-segment setpoint override
✓  Diluent / Oxygen / Bailout cylinder roles
✓  O₂ consumption + remaining duration at metabolic rate
✓  Scrubber endurance tracking across dives
✓  Bailout sufficiency check (independent or group)
✓  Loop density and diluent PPO₂ / EAD validation
Safety outputs
✓  CNS% and OTU oxygen-toxicity tracking
✓  TTS (time to surface) with ascent trigger guidance
✓  Tissue saturation (GF99) chart
✓  Critical warnings reviewed before diving
Planning tools

✓  Contingency plans (deeper / longer)

✓  Partial-pressure gas blending with heat compensation

USING IT OFFLINE (e.g. ON A BOAT)
AeroPlus Deco runs fully offline — no internet or mobile signal needed, so it works on a boat or anywhere out of coverage. The one thing to do first: start the app up while you still have a connection, so the latest decompression algorithms load onto your phone. Then keep it running in memory — leave it open in the background rather than force-quitting it — and it will plan dives normally with no coverage at all.
