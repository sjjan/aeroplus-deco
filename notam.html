About AeroPlus NOTAM

AeroPlus NOTAM fetches NOTAMs for an airport and presents them in two forms: the raw ICAO-formatted message and a plain-English translation that expands Q-codes and contractions so that non-pilot staff can quickly understand what is affected.

Data source: FAA External NOTAM API (international coverage). Without credentials, the app runs in demo mode using built-in sample NOTAMs for common African airports.

The translator is heuristic. It covers most standard ICAO contractions and Q-code categories but may not capture every nuance. The raw NOTAM is always shown alongside so operators can verify.

Not for navigation. Always verify with the official AIS before flight.
